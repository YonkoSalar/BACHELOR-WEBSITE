PROJECT

ICARUS

Breaking Limits

PROJECT

ICARUS is bachelor group at University of southeastern Norway in Norway. In collaboration with FFI, our goal is to research and develop alternatives ways of increasing the flight time of drones. Our goal is to explore some of these unique methods and design, to create a fully functional drone. We believe with our mutlidisiplinary group we will test our skills and abilities will in each discipline of engineering field.
