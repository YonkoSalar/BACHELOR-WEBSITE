PROJECT

ICARUS

Breaking Limits

PROJECT

ICARUS is bachelor group at University of southeastern Norway in Norway. In collaboration with FFI, our goal is to investigate and study the effect of aerodynamic on UAV to help establish new facts and research conclusions.
